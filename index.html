This website is regenerated by Travis Cron to Github Pages every 24 hours, so meetups are always fresh.

We show meetups for next 30 days, so you can pick up where to go speak or watch without being overwhelmed.

Why Abroad Meetups Rock?

Meeting people from other countries brings fresh energy to your coding, new ideas and valuable feedback.

You'll be surprised how easy is to get your talk there. Pick meetup, write the organizer and go!
